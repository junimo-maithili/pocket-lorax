What YOU Can Do to Help Out Our Planet

What You Can Do Information
Reduce Energy Usage Simple things like this can create a drastic impact on the Earth. Start this by turning off the lights when you leave the room, take colder showers, switch to fluorescent light bulbs.
Transportation Instead of taking your car to work, switch to other alternatives such as taking public transit (e.g. bus), walking, or biking.
Use Energy Efficient Appliances Oftentimes, they use 20% less energy, this means they are a game-changer to your bills and also the environment.
Recycle, Recycle This can help repurpose items, therefore cutting down waste.
Know who you are buying from. Knowing the companies/corporations (e.g. what materials they use, how do they get those materials, etc.) you are buying from can help you be more educated decision about how you impacting the environment.
